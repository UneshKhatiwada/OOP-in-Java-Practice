Interthread communication 

Inter thread communication or co-operation is all about allowing synchronized threads to communicate witn each other.
It is mechanism in which a thread is paused running  in its  critical section and another thread is allowed to enter in
the same critical section to be executed its implemented by following methods of object class.
wait()
notify()
notifyAll()
